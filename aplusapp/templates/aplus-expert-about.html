Frequently Asked Questions

How do we get started?

We provide half an hour of expert advise to all our new students all free of charge and with zero commitments. Our goal is to learn more about the students' needs and create a personalized plan to achieve their full potential. Afterwards, we'll lay down a plan of weekly sessions to improve the students' performance across the needed subjects.

How do we gaurantee results?

We're all about measurements and we continously work with our students and tutors to make sure we deliver specific tutoring to achieve the desired results. We regularly measure the performance of our students and modify strategies to assure success.

What grades can we come to A+ Expert for?

While we work with K-12 students of all stages, we heavily specialize in grade 9-12 students with a focus on preparing students for admission at top ranking universities in Canada, US and Europe.

How was A+ Expert started?

We're a team of experienced tutors who are currently top-achieving pre-med and engineering students at UBC. We love helping other students achieve their full academic potential, and we're honoured to have been trusted with so many students so far.
